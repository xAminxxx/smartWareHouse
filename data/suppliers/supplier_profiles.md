# Supplier Profiles & Reliability

## GlobalTech Supplies (Supplier of Electronics)

- **Reliability:** 95%
- **Typical Lead Time:** 2 days.
- **Notes:** High-value items. Always requires unloading at Gate A (Climate Controlled).
- **History:** Rarely late. Drivers are familiar with the site.

## CleanStep Co. (Supplier of Cleaning Products)

- **Reliability:** 70%
- **Typical Lead Time:** 5 days.
- **Notes:** Bulk items. Prone to spills. Requires Gate C.
- **History:** Often late by 1-2 hours. Vehicles often require extra time for paperwork.

## OfficeMax (Supplier of Stationery)

- **Reliability:** 85%
- **Notes:** Fast unloading. Can use any available Gate.
- **History:** Consistent performance.
