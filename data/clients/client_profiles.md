# Client Profiles & Priorities

## City Schools (Public Sector Client)

- **Priority:** High
- **Type:** Pickup (Outbound)
- **Notes:** Usually requires a lot of stationery. Prefers loading at Gate D.
- **History:** Reliable client, always has paperwork ready.

## RetailCorp (Commercial Client)

- **Priority:** Medium
- **Type:** Pickup (Outbound)
- **Notes:** High-volume orders of electronics. Requires forklift assistance.
- **History:** Sometimes arrives earlier than scheduled.
